Crystal Collector

A random number will be generated at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below with randomized values at the start of each game. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the number displayed under goal, you lose the game if your total score goes above the goal number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
